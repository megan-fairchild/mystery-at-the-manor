The Wardrobe

Dress the part - or just dress comfortably

Character Color Guide

Pick a Clue character and wear their signature color. Costumes are encouraged but not required - even just a solid-color shirt counts!

Miss Scarlet - Red dress, red top, or anything bold and crimson.
Colonel Mustard - Yellow or mustard tones. A blazer, polo, or button-down.
Mrs. Peacock - Royal blue or teal. Elegant, put-together.
Professor Plum - Purple, plum, or lavender. Maybe a bow tie.
Mr. Green - Green - forest, emerald, or olive.
Mrs. White - White or cream. Classic, composed.

Weather-Smart Layers

Warm at arrival (~74F) but cools off fast after sunset (~55F). Plan for both.

Arrival (6:00 PM): Comfortable warm-weather clothing. Your character color top.
Post-Sunset (8:00+ PM): Bring a jacket, hoodie, or flannel for the movie.
Footwear: Closed-toe shoes recommended (grass + games). Skip the heels unless you're fully committed to Miss Scarlet.

What to Bring

Lawn chair or camping chair
Cozy blanket for the movie
Warm layer (jacket, hoodie, flannel)
Bug spray (just in case)
Your character-color outfit
Optional: costume accessories (hat, boa, magnifying glass)
