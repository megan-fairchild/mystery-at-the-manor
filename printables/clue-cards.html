Clue Cards

Print, cut, and hide these around the yard in colored envelopes

Scarlet Envelope

"The thief was NOT wearing blue."

This eliminates Mrs. Peacock as a suspect.

Mustard Envelope

"The weapon is NOT the candlestick and NOT the rope."

Only a few weapons remain...

Peacock Envelope

"The trophy was hidden somewhere with WATER nearby."

Think: where is there water outside?

Plum Envelope

"The thief's favorite color is GREEN."

Which suspect likes green?

Green Envelope

"The weapon is something you use in the KITCHEN."

Not a wrench, not a lead pipe...

White Envelope

"The hiding spot is OUTSIDE, not inside the house."

The trophy is somewhere in the yard.

Answer Key (for the host only!)

WHO: Mr. Green

WHERE: By the garden hose / outdoor faucet

WITH WHAT: The knife

Hide the golden trophy near your garden hose or outdoor faucet before guests arrive. Seal this answer in an envelope for the dramatic reveal.
