🔎 The Investigation - Mini Whodunit

🕑 7:45 - 8:15 PM (~30 min) 👥 All ages (designed for 6+) 🎲 Mystery Game

Someone has stolen Mr. Boddy's golden trophy! The suspects have scattered clues around the manor grounds. Work together to discover WHO did it, WHERE they hid it, and WHAT tool they used.

How It Works

  1. Setup: Before guests arrive, hide 6 color-coded envelopes around the yard (one per Clue character color: red, yellow, blue, purple, green, white).
  2. The Hunt: Guests split up and search for envelopes. Each contains a picture clue and a short written clue. The 6-year-old is the Lead Detective with a magnifying glass prop.
  3. The Evidence Board: Bring found envelopes back to the "evidence board" (a poster board or table). Lay out all clues.
  4. The Accusation: As a group, decide: WHO stole the trophy, WHERE they hid it, and WITH WHAT weapon. The Lead Detective announces the final answer.
  5. The Reveal: Open the sealed "Answer Envelope" to see if they got it right!

Sample Clues (Customize These)

  • 🔴 Red envelope: "The thief was NOT wearing blue." (eliminates Mrs. Peacock)
  • 🟡 Yellow envelope: "The weapon is NOT the candlestick." (picture of crossed-out candlestick)
  • 🔵 Blue envelope: "The trophy was hidden somewhere with WATER." (picture of a faucet/hose)
  • 🟣 Purple envelope: "The thief likes the color GREEN." (picture of green splotch)
  • 🟢 Green envelope: "The weapon is something you use in the KITCHEN." (picture of a wrench crossed out, knife circled)
  • ⚪ White envelope: "The hiding spot is OUTSIDE, not inside." (picture of a garden)

Answer: Mr. Green, with the knife, by the garden hose! (Hide the trophy near your garden hose or outdoor faucet.)

Materials Needed

  • ✓ 6 colored envelopes (red, yellow, blue, purple, green, white)
  • ✓ Printed clue cards (see printables)
  • ✓ A small "golden trophy" to hide (spray paint a dollar store trophy)
  • ✓ Magnifying glass prop for the Lead Detective
  • ✓ Sealed "Answer Envelope"
  • ✓ Optional: detective hat, evidence board (poster board)

📷 Photo Op

🎬 The Screening: CLUE (1985)

🕑 8:30 - ~10:10 PM 👥 All ages (rated PG) 🎦 Movie

The main event! Gather under the stars for the 1985 classic. Cozy up with blankets and popcorn as the mystery unfolds on the big screen.

Runtime: 97 minutes

Rating: PG (mild innuendo, slapstick comedy - great for all ages)

Fun fact: The theatrical release had three different endings shown in different theaters. The home release includes all three endings shown back-to-back.

Setup Needed

  • ✓ Projector + screen (already have)
  • ✓ Outdoor speakers for audio
  • ✓ Extension cords / power strip
  • ✓ Blankets and lawn chairs
  • ✓ Bug spray / citronella candles
  • ✓ String lights for ambiance (dim enough not to wash out screen)
